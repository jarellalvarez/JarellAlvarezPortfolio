    • Web Design / RedPin

    RedPin is a group project that was made to help fellow travllers plan trips have fun before it's started. We began creating wireframes and going back and forth on how we can achieve a seemless experience that would be simple to use for the end user. The result was a passionate term project that showed off the skills of each of our team members.

    We started out brainstorming ideas on different ways to solve the common issues for our target user. To find out who are intended users were, we needed to go back to the drawing boards and start with the basics. We began to carve out the users personas in order to gain key insights to who our target market is. Afterwards we began create a styleguide to ensure a consistent design through our whole app.
