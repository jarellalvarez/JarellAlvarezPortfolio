Storytelling / Monsox

We wanted to make a proof of concept to a new digital story with characters, plot etc. The process of creating a collaborative project was tough with weekly progress reports and independent workings. There were different teams for parts of the project and I was responsible for the entire storyline. I began brainstorming lots of ideas and was able to narrow down the type of plot and storyline with inspirations coming from Avatar the Last Airbender. We were able to launch the entire project and come to our end goals..
